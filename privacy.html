Privacy Policy

PenFinger respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Camera Usage

PenFinger uses the device camera solely to detect finger movement in real time for drawing on the screen. All camera processing is performed locally on your device using Apple’s Vision framework.

No images, videos, or camera data are recorded, saved, or transmitted.

Data Collection

PenFinger does not collect personal information, usage data, analytics, identifiers, or location data.

Third-Party Services

PenFinger does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s Privacy

PenFinger does not knowingly collect any data from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
